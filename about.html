About AVA Aligners

Pioneering the future of smile transformation

Our Story

AVA Aligners was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make premium teeth straightening accessible, comfortable, and effective for everyone. Founded by a team of experienced orthodontists and dental technology innovators, we recognized that traditional braces weren't meeting the needs of modern patients.

Through years of research, development, and clinical trials, we've created a clear aligner system that combines cutting-edge technology with personalized care. Our commitment to excellence has helped transform over 1 million smiles worldwide, earning the trust of thousands of dental professionals.

Today, AVA Aligners stands at the forefront of orthodontic innovation, continuously advancing our technology to provide patients with the most comfortable, effective, and aesthetically pleasing teeth straightening solution available.
