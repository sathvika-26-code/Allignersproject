How AVA Aligners Work

Your journey to a perfect smile, step by step

Simple, Effective, Life-Changing

AVA Aligners uses advanced technology and personalized care to straighten your teeth comfortably and effectively. Here's everything you need to know about the process from start to finish.

1

Initial Consultation

Your journey begins with a comprehensive consultation at one of our partner dental clinics. During this visit, your dentist will:

  • Examine your teeth and oral health
  • Discuss your smile goals and expectations
  • Determine if you're a good candidate for clear aligners
  • Answer all your questions about the treatment process
  • Provide a preliminary timeline and cost estimate
Duration: 30-45 minutes | Cost: Often complimentary or minimal consultation fee
2

3D Digital Scanning

If you're a good candidate, we'll create a precise digital model of your teeth using state-of-the-art technology:

  • Quick and comfortable intraoral scanning (no messy impressions!)
  • High-resolution 3D digital mapping of your teeth
  • Photographs of your teeth and smile from multiple angles
  • X-rays if needed for comprehensive planning
Duration: 15-20 minutes | Comfort: Completely painless and quick
3

Custom Treatment Plan

Our expert orthodontists create your personalized treatment plan using advanced AI technology:

  • AI-powered analysis of your dental structure
  • Precise calculation of tooth movements needed
  • 3D simulation showing your week-by-week progress
  • Preview your final smile before starting treatment
  • Board-certified orthodontist review and approval
Duration: 5-7 business days | Benefit: See your future smile before you commit
4

Manufacturing Your Aligners

Once you approve your treatment plan, we manufacture your complete set of custom aligners:

  • Precision 3D printing of dental models
  • Medical-grade, BPA-free thermoplastic material
  • Each aligner crafted to exact specifications
  • Quality control inspection of every aligner
  • Secure packaging and delivery to your dentist
Duration: 2-3 weeks | Quality: Each aligner undergoes rigorous testing
5

Receive Your Aligners

Return to your dentist to receive your aligners and begin your transformation:

  • Your dentist fits your first set of aligners
  • Receive detailed wearing and care instructions
  • Learn how to insert and remove your aligners
  • Get your complete aligner supply or first batch
  • Schedule your follow-up appointments
Duration: 45-60 minutes | Feeling: Excited to begin your journey!
6

Wear Your Aligners

The active phase of your treatment where you'll see progressive changes:

  • Wear aligners 20-22 hours per day
  • Remove only for eating, drinking (except water), and brushing
  • Switch to a new set every 7-14 days as directed
  • Track your progress with our mobile app
  • Notice gradual improvements week by week
Duration: 6-18 months typically | Tip: Consistency is key to great results
7

Regular Check-ups

Periodic visits ensure your treatment stays on track:

  • Visit your dentist every 6-8 weeks
  • Monitor progress and tooth movement
  • Address any concerns or questions
  • Make adjustments if needed
  • Receive your next batch of aligners
Frequency: Every 6-8 weeks | Duration: 15-30 minutes per visit
8

Treatment Complete

Celebrate your new smile and receive your retainers:

  • Final evaluation of your beautiful new smile
  • Professional photographs for your records
  • Custom retainers to maintain your results
  • Instructions for retainer wear and care
  • Long-term maintenance recommendations
Important: Wear retainers as directed to keep your perfect smile forever

What to Expect During Treatment

Comfort Level

Mild pressure when switching to new aligners is normal - it means they're working! Most discomfort subsides within 2-3 days.

Speech

A slight lisp may occur initially but typically resolves within a few days as you get used to wearing aligners.

Daily Routine

Remove aligners for meals and oral hygiene. Clean them twice daily with a soft brush and lukewarm water.

Visible Progress

You'll notice improvements within the first few months, with increasingly dramatic changes as treatment progresses.

Caring for Your Aligners

Simple maintenance for optimal results

Clean Daily

Rinse and brush aligners morning and night with lukewarm water

Avoid Hot Water

Hot water can warp the plastic - always use lukewarm or cool water

Store Safely

Keep aligners in their case when not wearing to prevent damage or loss

Brush Before Wearing

Always brush your teeth before putting aligners back in
